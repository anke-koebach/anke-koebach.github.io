<p style='text-align: justify;'> Knowledge is power. Using the internet, we are lucky to have so much information available in a matter of few clicks. Unfortunately there is a lot of junk or even deceiving information in the internet as well. Therefore, I here enlist a non-exhaustive collection of websites that may lead you to the light side of the force. </p>
